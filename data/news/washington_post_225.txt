THE DISTRICT

Man, woman killed

in pair of shootings

A man and a woman were killed in two separate shootings within 24 hours of each other in the District, police said.

On Tuesday evening, before 7:30 p.m., Derrick Thomas was killed in a double shooting in the 400 block of Condon Terrace SE. A ShotSpotter detected two barrages of gunfire, first 32 rounds and then eight, according to a police report.

Police said they found a second victim outside a building with a gunshot wound to his lower left leg. He was transported to a hospital.

Efforts to reach the family of Thomas, a 27-year-old from Southeast Washington, were not successful.

Just before 11:15 a.m. Wednesday, a 23-year-old woman was shot and killed in the 3700 block of Jamison Street NE, police said.

Kaijah McCoy was found with gunshot wounds on the fourth floor of an apartment building. She was pronounced dead at the scene.

Efforts to reach McCoy's family were not successful.

By Thursday morning, police had not made an arrest in either case.

- Emily Davies

MARYLAND

Man found dead in fire had been shot

Authorities said they found a man dead inside a Maryland apartment Wednesday after a fire and believe he died in an apparent homicide.

Prince George's County police identified the victim as Roger Neal, 56, of Hyattsville.

The fire was reported just after 2 p.m. at a garden-style apartment in the 6100 block of 42nd Avenue in the Hyattsville area, according to authorities.

Prince George's police said firefighters found a man who was injured, and pronounced him dead at the scene. He had been shot, police said.

The case remains under investigation.

- Dana Hedgpeth
