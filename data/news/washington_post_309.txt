THE DISTRICT

Man fatally shot in Northeast Washington

A man was fatally shot Thursday afternoon in the Brentwood neighborhood of Northeast Washington, according to D.C. police.

The shooting occurred shortly after 1:30 p.m. in the 1300 block of Downing Street NE.

Police said officers responded to a report of gunshots and found the wounded man. Police said he was taken to a hospital, where he died. He was identified as Carlos Haythe, 21, of Washington.

No arrest has been made.

- Peter Hermann

MARYLAND

Teen charged in alleged family killing

A 17-year-old has been arrested and charged with murder in the killing of his family member in Prince George's County this month, police said.

The teen, of Washington, is charged as an adult in the fatal shooting of Jammi Cooper, 36, of Washington, police said.

Police, who did not name the teen, said that according to an initial investigation, the shooting stemmed from a dispute between the teen and Cooper.

- Jasmine Hilton

Three men charged in fatal March shooting

Three men have been arrested and charged with murder in a fatal shooting in March in Prince George's, police said.

Kenneth Gails, 30, of Riverdale; Marcus Chapman, 33, of Hyattsville; and Josue Vasques, 27, of Hyattsville, each are charged with first- and second-degree murder, conspiracy to commit murder and related counts in the killing of Jarell Thompson, 31, of Washington, police said. They are being held without bond at the county jail, according to police.

According to an initial investigation, the suspects knew Thompson and shot him during a robbery, police said.

- Jasmine Hilton
