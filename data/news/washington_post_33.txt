THE DISTRICT

56-year-old Md. man killed in SE shooting

A 56-year-old man was fatally shot early Tuesday in Southeast Washington, police said.

Michael Evans of Clinton died of gunshot wounds in the 600 block of 56th Place SE.

Police said they responded to a report of a shooting there about 12:45 a.m.

Authorities arrested Garry Denny, 50, who has no fixed address, and charged him with first-degree murder.

- Emily Davies

Police identify victims in two homicide cases

D.C. police have identified two victims in separate killings, one Thursday in Northeast and another Sunday in Southeast.

Orlando Galloway, 36, of Southeast Washington was killed about 7:30 p.m. Sunday in the 200 block of 14th Street SE. Officers responded to a report of an assault and found Galloway dead in an apartment from gunshot and stab wounds, police said. A woman in the apartment was taken to a hospital with stab wounds, police said.

Police arrested a suspect, Julius Worthy, 39, of Southeast Washington, on Monday. He was charged with second-degree murder while armed and assault with intent to kill, police said.

On Thursday, police responded to a triple shooting in the 600 block of Kenilworth Avenue NE. Officers found two injured men there and a third shortly after in Prince George's County. The three were transported to hospitals.

Sergio Nicolas Rosario Arias, 29, of the Bronx, died of his injuries Saturday, police said.

No arrest was reported in his death.

- Emily Davies
