THE DISTRICT

18-year-old dies of gunshot wounds

D.C. police are investigating the shooting death of an 18-year-old in Southeast Washington on Friday night.

About 10:15 p.m., officers responded to reports of a shooting in the 4000 block of Wahler Court SE. They learned that Shane Williams had suffered gunshot wounds and was being taken to a hospital by car, according to a release.

D.C. had recorded 244 homicides through Thursday, representing a 33 percent increase over the same period in 2022. The city has logged more killings this year than in any other year since 2003.

- Casey Parks

VIRGINIA

Man is fatally shot at hotel in Fairfax County

Fairfax County police are investigating a fatal shooting Sunday at a Marriott in Merrifield.

Officers responded just before 1 a.m. to the Marriott in the 3100 block of Fairfield Park Drive and found a man shot in the upper body. The victim was taken to a hospital but was later pronounced dead, police said.

Police detained two people but have released no further information.

- Casey Parks
