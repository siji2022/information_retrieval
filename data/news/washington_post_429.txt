THE DISTRICT

Man fatally shot in Southeast

A 37-year-old man was fatally shot Monday in Southeast Washington, police said.

Michael Ashby Jr. was shot and killed just after 1:50 p.m. in the 5000 block of Benning Road SE. Police said he had no fixed address.

Efforts to reach Ashby's family were not successful.

By Tuesday afternoon, authorities had not made an arrest in his killing.

- Emily Davies

MARYLAND

Woman slain in apartment

A 28-year-old woman was fatally shot inside her apartment Thursday in the unincorporated section of College Park, Prince George's County police said.

Officers at about 8:45 a.m. found Tiffany Ward unresponsive with trauma in her apartment in the 6200 block of Westchester Park Drive, police said. She was pronounced dead at the scene.

Police said an autopsy found that Ward had been shot and her death was ruled a homicide.

An investigation is ongoing. Efforts to reach Ward's relatives Tuesday afternoon were unsuccessful.

- Jasmine Hilton
