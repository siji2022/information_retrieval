THE DISTRICT

Man fatally shot in Southeast, police say

A 43-year-old man was fatally shot early Saturday morning in Southeast Washington, according to D.C. police.

At around 12:25 a.m., officers responded to a reported shooting at the 4000 block of 4th Street SE, a D.C. police spokesperson said.

Officers found an adult male victim dead in a hallway, the spokesperson said.

Police identified the victim as 43-year-old Antwoin Wilson of Southeast.

The block where the fatal shooting occurred is home to a number of apartment buildings, near the D.C.-Maryland line.

Police said the investigation was ongoing and no arrest had been made.
