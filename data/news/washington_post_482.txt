THE DISTRICT

1 killed, 3 wounded in shooting at nightclub

A 31-year-old man was fatally shot and three other people were wounded late Saturday at a nightclub in Northeast Washington, police said Sunday.

Blake Bozeman of Southeast Washington died following the incident, during which gunfire broke out shortly before midnight inside a club in the 1300 block of H Street NE, according to police.

Bozeman was taken to a hospital, where he died, police said. Two other men and a woman who were also hospitalized were being treated Sunday for wounds that were not considered life-threatening, according to police.

Police are searching for the gunman, who was last seen going west on H Street.

So far this year, there have been almost 200 homicides in the District, about 30 percent more than last year at this time.

- Martin Weil

Man is found fatally shot in Southeast

D.C. police are investigating the homicide of a 24-year-old man found shot inside a residence along 22nd Street SE, officials said.

Just after 6 p.m. Thursday, officers responded to the call of an unconscious person inside the home.

They found Amar Harris, of Southeast, who was soon pronounced dead.

- Dan Morse
